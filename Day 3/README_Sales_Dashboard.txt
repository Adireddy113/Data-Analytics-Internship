
# Sales Financial Dashboard - Power BI Project

## 📊 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive overview of sales financial data including quantity sold, customer analysis, and city-wise distribution. It's designed to help business stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 📁 Files Included
1. **Sales_Financial_Dashboard_PPT.pptx** - PowerPoint presentation summarizing the dashboard design, KPIs, and recommendations.
2. **Screenshot 2025-06-26 214204.png** - A snapshot of the interactive dashboard created in Power BI.

---

## 🧩 Dashboard Features
- **Line Chart**: Shows quantity sold by day for trend analysis.
- **Bar Charts**: Display city-level sales and customer types.
- **Card**: Total count of customer types by gender.
- **Treemap**: Combines rating and quantity by city.
- **Slicers**: Enable filtering by city, rating, and total.
- **Dark Theme**: Used for visual consistency and clarity.

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- Naypyitaw leads in total sales quantity.
- There are 20 customer types segmented by gender.
- Member customers contribute more to total sales than normal customers.
- Daily sales have noticeable peaks and dips.

---

## ✅ Recommendations
- Focus on underperforming regions like Mandalay and Yangon.
- Promote loyalty programs to convert normal to member customers.
- Add profitability metrics to provide a complete financial view.
- Use drillthrough and tooltips to enhance interactivity.
